Skin Cancer


What is Cancer?


Cancer is the cells in your body going through mitosis, and they will keep on going through mitosis faster than the regular cells.


Types of Skin Cancer:


  • Actinic keratoses
  • Basal cell carcinoma
  • Squamous Cell carcinoma
  • Melanoma


What are Treatments for Skin Cancer?


Treatments for skin cancer include surgery, chemotherapy, radiation treatment, and other treatments. Other treatments could include Photodynamic Therapy, Biologic Therapy, Targeted Therapy


How do you Prevent Skin Cancer?


  • Seek shade, around 10 AM to 4 PM
  • Avoid tanning or tanning in UV beds
  • Cover your skin up with clothes and extras
  • Use broad spectrum sunscreen
  • Apply one ounce of sunscreen 30 minutes before going out
  • Examine your skin everywhere every month
  • See a physican every year
